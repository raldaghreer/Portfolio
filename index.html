Medical Approval Notification Time Analysis

This project involves analyzing medical approval notification data to identify causes of delays and improve response times.

Tools Used: Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn), SQL, Excel, Power BI.

Methodology: Data cleaning and statistical analysis combined with visualizations were applied to pinpoint timeframes causing delays in notification processes.

Results: Recommendations were made that reduced average notification time by 25%, significantly enhancing medical operational efficiency.
